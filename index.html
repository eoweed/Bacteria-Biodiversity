The gauge chart shows how many times a week the subject washes their belly button.
The bar chart shows the top 10 most abundant bacteria found in the belly button of the subject. Hovering over each bar will highlight the bacteria OTU ID, and display the common names of each bacteria and the number of bacteria found.
The bubble chart shows the number of bacteria cultures per each type of bacteria. The bigger the bubble means a higher number of bacteria cultures were found. Hovering over the bubbles will show the OTU ID, the number of samples found, and the common name for that type of bacteria.
